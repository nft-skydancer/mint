About Skydancer

Walking around the mall or heading to the parking lot, you've probably noticed Sky Dancers. But you never paid much attention to them. And very, very wrong. You have to ask, "Why?" It's simple! Behind each of them is a story. Yes, yes, they are living beings who also spend time with their loved ones, fall in love, find new hobbies. It's just like people! But there is one difference! They are not bogged down in the usual routine. They live each day as if it were their last.

Our goal is to convey the lightness and carefree nature of the real world. After all, very often all of us forget about the simple pleasures of life, and there are many of them - you only need to expand your horizons! The main characters are like saying, "Stop, you can't go on like this. After all, there are so many beautiful things around, and we are immersed in our own problems". Each work is the embodiment of happy moments. Its bright colors just can not help but charge a positive mood, inspiration and energy for the next day.

What are the advantages of this project? When you holder our NFT, you become a part of a huge community. In addition, we haven't forgotten about the bonuses, which you will learn about a little later.
